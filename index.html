About

IT Camp was established since 24th January,2014 and this year is fourth time.

IT Camp is free to attend locally organized “unconferences” where the guest speakers and participants are allowed to present about Information Technology.

IT Camp is similar to Barcamp but it allows to present only IT and related field.

Organizers are only required to take care of promotion, logistics, and infrastructure for the event while attendees proactively present and choose their own content.
