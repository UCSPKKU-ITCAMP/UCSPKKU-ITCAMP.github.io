Information

There are two types of people, the guest speaker and the participant. During the event, the topics of the guest speaker will be announced on the notice board. The participants can get the information on that notice board and on our official website. They can go to the room and discuss the topic what they are interested.

About

IT Camp will be held on 23th August,2018 for the first time. IT Camp is free to attend locally organized “unconferences” where the guest speakers and participants are allowed to present about Information Technology. IT Camp is similar to Barcamp but it allows to present only IT and related field. Organizers are only required to take care of promotion, logistics, and infrastructure for the event while attendees proactively present and choose their own content.
